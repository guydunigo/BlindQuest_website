Introduction

BlindQuest is a little Dungeon Crawler in which a sharp eye will be much cumbersome compared to a keen ear.

What ?!?

Slay, delve, loot... But beware ruffian ! Listen carefully and you will dodge numerous dangers and eventually you shall be granted treasures and glory.
You must also be aware that you are scared to death as soon as you touch water.
Grab your helmet, close your eyes and bon voyage !

Keyboard/Magic ?

<^>` Move around
E Attack during figths
H Show/Hide help message
P Pause/Continue the game
F Enable/Disable fullscreen
S Save your progression
C Load a savefile
ECHAP Quit the game

Why ?

BlindQuest was designed during spring 2015 in connection with a coding project in the ENIB (École Nationale d'Ingénieurs de Brest). We wanted to create a game beyond usual expreriences with a unique gameplay.
Bonus, even blind people can play!
